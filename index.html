About Us

Fresh Coffee

Our coffee comes from Planet Bean, a small fair trade/organic micro-roaster located in Guelph, Ontario.

Fresh Ingredients

Our specialty is providing breakfast & lunch made with fresh ingredients, locally raised meats and free run eggs.

Made With Love

We make everything in house and infuse it with love! Including our bread, baked goods, soup, quiche, baked French toast, all our sauces and berry jam.
